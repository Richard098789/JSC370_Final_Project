Visualizations

These two plots now follow the same preparation and plotting logic used in final.qmd.

XGBoost actual vs predicted salary. Points near the dashed 45-degree line indicate accurate predictions, while larger vertical gaps highlight jobs where predicted salary differs most from observed salary.

Role × province treemap. Rectangle area represents posting count and color encodes the median annual salary midpoint, allowing quick comparison of both market size and pay level across role-region combinations.

Salary distribution by classified role. Each box summarizes the interquartile range and median salary, while outlier points reveal unusually high or low postings within each role.

Average salary trend over time by role. The monthly lines help compare whether role categories move together or diverge as posting dates progress.

Interactive summary table of role-level market statistics, including posting volume and median salary midpoint, to complement the graphical views with exact values.
